Frequently Asked Questions

Dhaka, the capital of Bangladesh, is a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and rapid development. It's one of the largest cities in South Asia and serves as the economic, political, and cultural hub of the country.

The cost of attending various activities or institutions in Dhaka, Bangladesh, varies widely: Universities: Private university tuition ranges from BDT 100,000 to BDT 500,000 per semester; public universities are cheaper, at BDT 5,000 to BDT 50,000. Tourist Attractions: Entrance fees for places like Lalbagh Fort and Ahsan Manzil are around BDT 20-200 for locals and BDT 100-200 for foreigners. Events: Concerts and cultural events range from BDT 500 to BDT 5,000. Dining: Meals cost BDT 100-500 at local eateries and BDT 1,000-5,000 at fine dining restaurants. Hotels: Budget hotels range from BDT 1,000 to BDT 3,000 per night, mid-range hotels from BDT 3,000 to BDT 7,000, and luxury hotels from BDT 10,000 to BDT 20,000.
